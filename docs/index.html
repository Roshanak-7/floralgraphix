About me

Graphic Designer

My name is Roshanak Ghadiry

I am an experienced Graphic Designer with a reputation for innovative thinking, creativity, and a solid technical skillset. My job is to deliver effective design solutions and drive business success. I have worked extensively with graphic software including Adobe Photoshop, Adobe Illustrator, Adobe InDesign, Adobe Acrobat, Adobe Dreamweaver, HTML, CSS, PowerPoint and more. I invite you to browse my portfolio to see examples. I'm also excited to share my hand drawn artworks with you on my Instagram page! I love to create joyful floral arts and nature using pencil, color pencil, ink, watercolor on paper and on my iPad in ProCreate. Please visit my page and follow me!
